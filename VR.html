SYMPOSIUM FOR ARTISTIC RESEARCH / SWEDISH RESEARCH COUNCIL

The Swedish Research Council annual symposium was held in Malmö and arranged in collaboration with Malmö University, Inkonst and Inter Art Cente. I was asked to design the program for the symposium.

I was the graphic designer in charge of concept, design and layout for the program. The program needed to have a clear easily read design The symposium was arranged as a hybrid event, which required extra attention to the schedule of the program to make it readable for both onsite and online participants.
