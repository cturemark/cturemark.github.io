freelance // typeface designer

Overview This company is in the process of a re-branding and was in need
of their own typeface. I was commissioned to make the typeface and was
given a sketch of a typeface to work from.

Challenge As a freelancer with limited hours, put myself into the company enough
to understand how the details of each letter would align with their vision.

My role My responsibility was to edit each letter, find a rhythm in the font
and make sure it technically worked to install on both a Mac and a PC.
