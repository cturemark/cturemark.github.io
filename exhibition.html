GRADUATION EXHIBITION

Every year, the graduating Graphic Design class from Malmö University gets to exhibit
their thesis work at Form/Deign Centre in Malmö. The class of 2020 graduates, in a time when
social distancing has placed a screen between ourselves and the outside world, had to rethink
the idea of a physical exhibition. This is the result of our virtual exhibition(s). The exhibition
was put together and organized by three students and our supervisors. We - the students – were
responsible for the main organization, website and poster design, text editing for all the individual
projects, video editing and last but not least printing the exhibition poster on a Risograph.

As the theme for the exhibition, we used a future yet retro aspect of screens to highlight
how they connect and divide us. The screens are a rectangular rabbit holes; through which we
collaborate, stay informed, manage our studies, mediate daily life, stream series and spend time
with near and dear ones, all within a gleaming light.

The website and showcased works have been appreciated by students and teachers from the
university as well as acknowledged and appreciated by others.
