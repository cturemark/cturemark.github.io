STORMATHON / MALMÖ UNIVERSITY


Background - Stormathon is a recurring student event at Malmö University organized by the innovation environment Storm where I was employed during this project. An external client contributes with an assignment and the students work in groups for 12h.

My role was to design material for marketing the event, and find suitable channels to market the material Storm’s and the university’s digital channels and physical locations.
