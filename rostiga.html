MAP ILLUSTRATIONS / ROSTIGA ROADTRIPS

I was a part of the team that made the title sequence for the TV series Rostiga Roadtrips,
airing on SVT in 2024. My role was to illustrate the maps. We needed to draw the
maps from scratch to have greater freedom in the animation phase. This required extreme
precision as some maps overlapped and needed scalability.
